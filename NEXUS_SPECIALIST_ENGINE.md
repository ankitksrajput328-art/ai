# 🎭 Nexus AI: Ultra-Specialist & Agent Engine

The Nexus Ultra-Specialist Engine transforms the base AI into a fleet of highly trained, autonomous experts and companions.

---

## 1. Specialist Personalities (Expert Mode)
Each specialist is powered by a **Deep-System Prompt (DSP)** and a specialized **Knowledge Base (RAG)**.

### A. The Companion (Emotionally Aware)
- **Goal:** Empathy, long-term emotional memory, and companionship.
- **Logic:** Uses **Sentiment Analysis** to adapt its tone (comforting, playful, or supportive).

### B. The Professionals (Doctor, Lawyer, Tutor)
- **AI Doctor Assistant:** Focused on triage and medical data analysis (non-diagnostic).
- **AI Lawyer Assistant:** Specialized in document drafting and legal research.
- **AI Tutor:** Uses the **Socratic Method** to lead students to answers rather than giving them away.

---

## 2. Autonomous Agent Framework (The "Do-er")
Unlike standard chatbots, **Nexus Agents** can take actions in the real world.

### A. Browser Operator (Web Agency)
- **Stack:** Playwright + Vision LLM.
- **Function:** Nexus can "see" a website, find the "Buy" button, and complete a transaction or research task autonomously.

### B. Workflow Automation (Zero-Code)
- **Logic:** "Nexus, find the top 5 news articles on AI today, summarize them, and post them to my LinkedIn."
- **Execution:** The AI builds its own task graph and executes it using external APIs.

---

## 3. Specialized Assistants
- **AI Coding IDE:** Integrated terminal and code execution environment for real-time debugging.
- **AI Gaming Assistant:** Real-time strategy advice and walkthroughs based on game-state analysis.
- **AI Social Media Creator:** Automated video scripting, image generation, and caption writing.

---

**Architect's Note:** We don't just "switch prompts." Each specialist has access to a unique set of **Tools** (e.g., the Lawyer has access to a Legal Database, the Coder has access to a Python Sandbox).
